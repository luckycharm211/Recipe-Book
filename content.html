Panner Butter Masala

Panneer Butter Masala

    Ingredients

    For Paneer Marinade:

  • 250 grams of paneer (cottage cheese), cut into cubes
  • 1/4 cup yogurt (curd)
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste

    • For the Gravy:

  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 2 large tomatoes, pureed
  • 1/2 cup tomato paste
  • 1/4 cup cashew nuts, soaked in warm water
  • 1/2 cup fresh cream
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar (optional)
  • Salt to taste

    Process

  • Step:1 Marinating the Paneer:

    • In a bowl, mix yogurt, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Add the paneer cubes to this mixture and coat them evenly,Let the paneer marinate for about 15-20 minutes.

  • Making the Cashew Paste:

    • Grind soaked cashew nuts into a smooth paste with some water and set aside.

  • Step 3: Cook the Paneer

    • Heat a pan, add butter and oil. Cook marinated paneer until light golden brown and remove and set aside.

  • Step 4: Prepare the Gravy

    • In the same pan, add butter.Sauté garlic and ginger, then add chopped onions until golden.

  • Step 5: Add Tomato Puree

    • Add tomato puree and cook until oil separates (about 5-7 minutes).Stir in tomato paste and cook for 2-3 minutes.

  • Step 6: Create Creamy Base

    • Mix in cashew paste, red chili powder, garam masala, sugar (if using), and salt.

  • Step 7: Finish and Serve

    • Pour in fresh cream, adjust consistency with water, and add the cooked paneer.Simmer for a few minutes,Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve hot with naan or rice.
